Myths
Co-op, Action RPG
Senior Technical Level Designer

September 2023 - May 2026

Myths is a fast-paced action RPG and four-player dungeon crawler where ancient mythologies collide. Players battle through shifting
realms—from Norse tundras to Mayan temples—hunting down legendary bosses. The core gameplay is driven by a unique absorption mechanic: as
players defeat these mythical foes, their characters and gear physically and mechanically absorb the boss's powers, allowing for evolving,
highly personalized combat builds.

Once I joined the team I was the sole level designer and had to set up the level pipeline mostly from scratch.

Responsibilities
	* Blocking out all the playable space for the game as the sole level designer for the project.
	* Art collaboration - Together with the environment art team and tech art figure out how technically what was required to hit the art requirements.
	* Pipeline - Responsible for figuring out and setting up the level design pipeline.
	* Tech requirements - Together with the tech team 
	* Long term solution - Procedural solution
