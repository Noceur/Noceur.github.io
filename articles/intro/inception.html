Welcome. I am a Technical Level Designer focused on optimizing content generation workflows and building custom toolsets for virtual world building. I'm very passionate about coherent world building.

I’ve been in the industry for a decade, and have had the privilege of working on projects like Albion Online, Homeworld Mobile, and Myths. My work has spanned from hands-on level design to architecting pipelines that empower teams to scale content efficiently.

I strongly believe in automating away repetitive tasks and generally reducing the overhead of content creation. When making the game becomes fun, design and creativity can flourish. When it becomes a slog, it can be hard to stay motivated and the quality suffers.

Other than general level design my priority is designing pipelines and tools that empower my colleagues to scale content. Having spent years at studios like Sandbox Interactive (Albion Online), Stratosphere Games (Homeworld Mobile), and Spectarium Games (Myths), I’ve directly experienced where pipelines choke.

My philosophy revolves around architectural honesty — if code or structural patterns are redundant, build a script to automate it. This site is created to serve as an interactive record of my professional work, procedural tinkering, and other experiments.
