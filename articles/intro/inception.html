Digital Logbook Inception

Welcome. I am a Technical Level Designer focused on optimizing content generation workflows and building custom programmatic toolsets for virtual world building.

Other than general level design my priority is designing pipelines and tools that empower my colleagues to scale content. Having spent years at studios like Sandbox Interactive (Albion Online), Stratosphere Games (Homeworld Mobile), and Spectarium Games (Myths), I’ve directly experienced where pipelines choke.

My philosophy revolves around architectural honesty — if code or structural patterns are redundant, build a script to automate it. This site is created to serve as an interactive record of my professional work, procedural tinkering, and experiments.
