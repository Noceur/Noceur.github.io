GGJ 2025 - Sonosphere 27.01.2025

I participated in the Global Game Jam 2025 with a team of 4 people, and we made a small game called Sonosphere. You navigate through levels by figuring out sound based puzzles which expands the bubble for how much of the level can be seen. The game was made using Unity and with a little bit of help from Houdini for environments and blender for other modeling. We had a lot of fun making the game and we are really happy with the result. The game won an award from the jam location for "theme". You can check out the game here.

There is also a short recording the game which you can watch on youtube.
