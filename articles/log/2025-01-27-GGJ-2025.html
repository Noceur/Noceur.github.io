27.01.2025

I participated in the Global Game Jam 2025 with a team of 4 people, and we made a game called Sonosphere. The game is a rhythm based puzzle platformer where you have to navigate through different levels by jumping on platforms that are generated based on the music. The game was made in Unity and we used a lot of different tools and assets to create the game. We had a lot of fun making the game and we are really happy with the result.
