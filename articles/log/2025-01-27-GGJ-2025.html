GGJ 2025 - Sonosphere 27.01.2025

I participated in the Global Game Jam 2025 as part of a team of 4, and we made a small game called Sonosphere, which won in the category "Theme". In Sonosphere you navigate through levels by solving sound based puzzles. Each solved puzzle increases the size of the bubble in which the game takes place, and reveals more of the level to the player. The game was made using Unity, with a little bit of help from Houdini for environments, and blender for other modeling. We had a lot of fun making the game and were really happy with the result. here.
